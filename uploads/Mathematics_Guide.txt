===== MATHEMATICS EDUCATIONAL RESOURCE =====

ALGEBRA FUNDAMENTALS
- Linear Equations: ax + b = c, solving for x
- Quadratic Equations: ax² + bx + c = 0, using quadratic formula
- Polynomial Operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication
- Factorization: GCF, trinomial factoring, difference of squares
- Exponent Laws: product rule, quotient rule, power rule
- Radical Expressions: simplifying, rationalizing denominators

GEOMETRY ESSENTIALS
- Properties of Triangles: angles sum to 180°, Pythagorean theorem
- Circle Geometry: radius, diameter, circumference, area
- Volume and Surface Area: cubes, spheres, cylinders, cones
- Transformations: translations, rotations, reflections, dilations
- Coordinate Geometry: distance formula, midpoint formula

CALCULUS BASICS
- Limits: understanding limits at a point and at infinity
- Derivatives: power rule, product rule, chain rule
- Applications: rates of change, optimization problems
- Integration: antiderivatives, definite integrals
- Area Under Curves: using integration

STATISTICS AND PROBABILITY
- Data Analysis: mean, median, mode, standard deviation
- Probability: basic probability, compound events, conditional probability
- Distributions: normal distribution, binomial distribution
- Hypothesis Testing: confidence intervals, significance tests
- Regression Analysis: linear regression, correlation coefficients

KEY FORMULAS
- Area Circle: A = πr²
- Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²
- Quadratic Formula: x = [-b ± √(b²-4ac)] / 2a
- Distance Formula: d = √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]
- Midpoint Formula: M = [(x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2]

PRACTICE QUESTIONS
Q1: Solve 2x + 5 = 13
Q2: Find the area of a circle with radius 7 cm
Q3: Use the quadratic formula to solve x² + 3x - 10 = 0
Q4: Calculate the distance between points (3,4) and (6,8)
Q5: What is the probability of rolling a 6 on a standard die?
